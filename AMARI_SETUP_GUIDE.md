## Step-by-Step Setup Instructions

### 1. Access Theme Customizer
1. Go to **Shopify Admin** > **Online Store** > **Themes**
2. Click **Customize** on your theme
3. Navigate to the **Home** page

### 2. Hero Section (Amari Hero)

**Upload Images:**
- **Oval Image 1** (Top Left): Small portrait image of skincare application
- **Oval Image 2** (Left): Medium portrait of person using product
- **Oval Image 3** (Center - Main): Large oval portrait - main hero image (woman with skincare)
- **Oval Image 4** (Right): Small product shot or person image

**Add Products:**
- Click **Add block** > **Product Card** (add 2 cards)
- Select featured products to showcase

**Recommended Image Sizes:**
- Oval Image 1: 400x500px
- Oval Image 2: 300x400px
- Oval Image 3: 800x1000px (Main focal point)
- Oval Image 4: 350x450px

### 3. Word Cloud Section
✅ Already configured with keywords (Luminescent, Youthful, Soft, Dewy, Smooth, Vibrant, Radiance, Glow)

### 4. Our Collections Section

**Add Collections:**
1. Click on the **Amari Collections** section
2. For each block (4 total), select a collection:
   - Cleansing Essentials
   - Skin Hydration
   - Anti-Ageing
   - Skin Exfoliator

**Tip:** Make sure each collection has a featured image set

### 5. Exclusive Products Section

**Upload Images:**
- **Main Product Image**: Product lineup photo (bottles, jars, etc.)
- **Badge Image**: Gold seal or certificate badge (appears over main image)

**Add Feature Icons:**
- Upload 5 small icons (64x64px) for each feature
- Examples: Leaf icon, Water drop, Shield, etc.

### 6. Best Sellers Section

**Select Collection:**
- Choose your "Best Sellers" or main product collection
- Set "Products to Show": 8-12 products

**Important:** Make sure products have:
- Featured images
- Prices set
- Compare at prices (for "Sale" badges)

### 7. Promotional Banners

**For each of 4 banners:**
1. Upload background image
2. Set discount badge (e.g., "30% Off")
3. Add heading and subheading
4. Link to relevant collection/product
5. Choose text color and alignment

**Recommended Layout:**
- Banner 1: Product combo offer (light background)
- Banner 2: Limited time offer (pink/light background)
- Banner 3: Anti-aging product (dark background with product image)
- Banner 4: Brightening cream (light background)

### 8. Expertise/Stats Section

**Upload Images:**
- **Image 1** (Top Left): Square image of person using product
- **Image 2** (Top Right): Square image of skincare routine
- **Image 3** (Bottom): Wide rectangle of product/lifestyle shot

**Stats are already configured:**
- 8K+ Global Customers
- 12K+ Products Delivered
- 10+ Years In Business

### 9. News & Blogs Section

**Setup:**
1. Click on **Amari Blogs** section
2. Select your blog (e.g., "News" or "Blog")
3. Make sure you have at least 3 published blog posts
4. Each post should have a featured image

### 10. Testimonials Section
✅ Already configured with 3 sample testimonials

**To customize:**
- Upload customer photos
- Edit customer names and locations
- Update testimonial text

### 11. Instagram Section

**Upload Images:**
- Add 6 Instagram post images (6 blocks)
- Each should be square (1:1 ratio)
- Link each to actual Instagram posts

**Settings:**
- Add your Instagram profile URL
- Customize hashtag (default: "#theAmari")

### 12. Footer (Amari Footer)
✅ Already configured with brown theme

**Customize:**
- Upload your logo
- Update contact information
- Set social media links in **Theme Settings** > **Social Media**

## Quick Tips for Best Results

### Image Quality
- Use high-resolution images (at least 1200px width)
- Optimize images before upload (use TinyPNG or similar)
- Maintain consistent color palette (warm tones, beiges, browns)

### Colors Used in Amari Design
- Background: `#FFF8F0` (cream)
- Buttons: `#92400E` (amber-800)
- Footer: `#4A2C2A` (brown)
- Accents: Amber and gold tones

### Content Guidelines
1. **Hero Section**: Focus on your best-selling or signature product
2. **Collections**: Show variety - different skin concerns
3. **Testimonials**: Use real customer feedback
4. **Banners**: Highlight current promotions or popular products

## Downloading Images from Amari

Since you have access to the Amari site (password: 1), you can:
1. Visit https://amari-theme.myshopify.com/
2. Right-click on images and save them
3. Use these as placeholders initially
4. Replace with your own product photos later

## Need Help?

If sections still look empty after adding content:
1. Click **Save** in the theme customizer
2. Refresh the preview
3. Check that products/collections are published
4. Ensure images are uploaded and not just linked

## Console Errors Explained

The 404 errors you're seeing are normal:
- **ShopifySans font**: This is a Shopify marketing font, doesn't affect your site
- **Favicon**: Add a favicon in Theme Settings > Favicon
- **CSP frame error**: This is a Shop app iframe, doesn't affect functionality

These errors don't impact the visual design or functionality of your theme.
